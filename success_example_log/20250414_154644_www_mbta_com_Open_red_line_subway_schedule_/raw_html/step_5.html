Alerts

Service

  • Service Change
    Red Line Ashmont Branch: Service between JFK/UMass and Ashmont will operate with two shuttle trains from April 10 - 30 to allow for critical track work. Riders will experience longer wait times for trains and should plan extra travel time. MBTA.com/RedLine
    Shuttle Train Information:
    A shuttle train is one train that will operate, back and forth, between Ashmont and JFK/UMass on a single track. Two shuttle trains will be operating between Ashmont and JFK/UMass during this work – one on each track. Shuttle trains will terminate at JFK/UMass. Riders will need to transfer from the Ashmont side of JFK/UMass station to the Braintree side for continued service towards Alewife, or southbound service to Braintree.

    Travel Times:
    Riders will experience longer wait times for trains during this work and should allow for extra travel time.

    Commuter Rail:
    Riders can connect to Fairmount Commuter Rail service by using bus route 22 from Ashmont to Talbot Ave station.
    Updated: 4/10/2025 2:30 AM EDT

Elevator & Escalator

  • Andrew Escalator Closure Ongoing
    Andrew Escalator 123 (Ashmont/Braintree platform to busway) is unavailable until further notice pending replacement.
  • Quincy Center Escalator Closure Ongoing
    Quincy Center Escalator 330 (Red Line platform to lobby) unavailable due to maintenance
  • Davis Escalator Closure Ongoing
    Davis Escalator 342 (Holland Street to unpaid lobby) unavailable due to maintenance
  • Harvard Escalator Closure
    Harvard Escalator 513 (Harvard Square to mezzanine) unavailable due to maintenance
  • South Station Escalator Closure
    South Station Escalator 420 (Alewife platform to South Station platform) unavailable due to maintenance
  • South Station Escalator Closure
    South Station Escalator 382 (Alewife platform to lobby) unavailable due to maintenance

Bike

  • South Station Bike Issue Ongoing
    South Station Pedal & Park is closed due to construction. Bicycle racks are currently available on Atlantic Ave adjacent to the South Station main entrance. MBTA.com/projects/south-station-transportation-center-improvements

Parking

  • North Quincy Parking Issue Ongoing
    North Quincy Newport Avenue Parking Lot closed beginning Wed Oct 16 due to construction. The vehicle entrance and exit will relocate to the old “state street” driveway. Public access via stairs/escalators to the lobby is restricted. Elevator access only.
  • Alewife Parking Issue Ongoing
    Alewife Garage: Beginning Monday, April 7, the vehicle exit will temporarily relocate to the third level. This is to accommodate ongoing construction in the garage.
